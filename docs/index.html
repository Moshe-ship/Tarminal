What's inside
ع
Arabic Letters
Connected letter forms via Core Text. Initial, medial, final — renders properly unlike iTerm2, Ghostty, or Alacritty.
◆
Metal GPU
Hardware-accelerated rendering on Apple Silicon. Smooth scrolling, low CPU, native performance.
◫
Tab Groups
Color-coded tabs. Drag to reorder. Right-click to assign colors. Cmd+1-9 to switch. Tabs persist on switch.
↓
Drag & Drop
Drop files onto the terminal. Paths are auto-escaped and pasted at the cursor position.
⟳
Session Restore
Quit and relaunch — your tabs, directories, and colors come back exactly where you left off.
⌘F
Find in Scrollback
Cmd+F to search terminal output. Cmd+G to find next, Cmd+Shift+G for previous.
◑
5 Themes
Dark, Light, Dracula, Nord, Sarab. Window transparency with vibrancy. Full ANSI 256 colors.
⚙
Every Setting Works
Cursor style, blink, scrollback, bell, dock bounce, Option-as-Meta, title bar. No fake toggles.
>_
Full Terminal
zsh, bash, fish. VT100/xterm via SwiftTerm. Clickable URLs. Works with Claude Code, vim, htop — everything.
